Account & Data Deletion

Headshot Air Battle
Last Updated: February 9, 2026

1. Request Account Deletion

You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. To submit a deletion request, please send an email to:

Email: skingway@gmail.com

Subject Line: Account Deletion Request - Headshot Air Battle

Please include the following information in your email so we can locate your account:

  • The email address associated with your Google Sign-In
  • Your in-game display name (if known)

2. Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon processing your deletion request, the following data will be permanently removed:

Data TypeDetails
Account InformationFirebase Authentication credentials, email, display name, profile photo URL, user ID
Game StatisticsScores, win/loss records, gameplay performance data
AchievementsAll unlocked achievements and progress
Leaderboard DataRankings and associated display names
Battle HistoryRecords of past matches and outcomes
In-App Purchase RecordsPurchase verification tokens stored on our servers

3. Data That May Be Retained

Certain data may be retained for a limited period as required by law or for legitimate business purposes:

  • Transaction records — Purchase history may be retained for up to 180 days for tax and legal compliance
  • Third-party data — Data collected by Google AdMob and Google Play is managed under their own retention policies. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details

4. Deletion Timeline

  1. We will acknowledge your request within 3 business days
  2. Identity verification may be required to protect your account
  3. Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of verification
  4. You will receive an email confirmation once the deletion is complete

5. Important Notes

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once your account is deleted:
  • All game progress, achievements, and statistics will be lost
  • Any purchased items or unlocked content cannot be restored
  • You will not be able to recover your account

6. Alternative: Delete Data from Your Device

If you only want to remove local data without deleting your cloud account, you can:

  • Clear App Data: Go to your device Settings > Apps > Headshot Air Battle > Storage > Clear Data
  • Uninstall the App: This will remove all locally stored preferences and cached data

Note: This will not delete your cloud-stored account data. To fully delete all data, please submit a deletion request as described above.
