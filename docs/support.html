Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Store in the app. Your purchases are linked to your Apple ID or Google account and will be automatically restored when you sign in on a new device. If purchases are not restored, try the "Restore Purchases" button in the Store.

How do I remove ads?

You can purchase the "Remove Ads" item from the in-app Store. This is a one-time purchase that permanently removes all banner and interstitial ads.

How does online multiplayer work?

You can play online through Quick Match (random opponent) or Private Room (share a room code with a friend). The game supports cross-platform play between iOS and Android.

How do I delete my account?

Please visit our Account Deletion page for instructions. You can also contact us via email to request account deletion.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Please email us at service@tybbtech.com with a description of the issue, your device model, and OS version. Screenshots or screen recordings are very helpful.
